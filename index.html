The Story Behind the Music

When the entertainment world went quiet — no stage lights, no live audience, no theatre — I went back to the drawing board, leaned into my Ugandan roots, and teamed up with Marlice Baart (co-writer) and DJ NLZ (producer). That is how Kyakuwa Music was born.

My name is Isaac Bullock Kintu. Kyakuwa is another name my mother gave me — it means "a gift from God."

I performed for a king when I was young, and the moment I tasted the stage, I was hooked. Sharing the stage with different artists keeps me learning new styles and pushing others to do the same.

"One Meter Fifty" — written by Isaac Bullock and Marlice Baart — won Best Social Causes Song of 2020 at the International Songwriting Competition.

Our Values

The Stage Is Big Enough For Everyone

From humble beginnings to a flourishing network, our dedication to authenticity and diversity creates unforgettable experiences.

Authenticity

Real music, real connections. No playback, no pretense. Every note is felt, every moment is genuine. We believe in the power of live, unfiltered expression.

Diversity

Bridging cultural divides through shared human experiences. Music is the universal language that connects us all — from Uganda to Amsterdam and beyond.

Elevation

Connecting the dots with music while adding value. We do not just entertain — we transform events into experiences that inspire and uplift.

From 40 Musicians to 800+
